About Me

Welcome to my academic portfolio. I am a dedicated researcher with extensive experience in multiple interdisciplinary fields including sports science, health research, social media analytics, and traffic studies.

My research focuses on the intersection of technology and human behavior, exploring how digital platforms influence various aspects of our daily lives. I am particularly interested in data-driven approaches to understanding complex social phenomena.

Through my work, I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications, contributing to evidence-based solutions for real-world challenges.
